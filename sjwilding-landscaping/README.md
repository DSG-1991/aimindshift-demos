## 🎯 Business Pain Points Addressed

This demo website solves the following critical business challenges:

### 1. **Poor Online Visibility**
- **Problem:** Customers can't find the business when searching for landscaping services
- **Solution:** SEO-optimized website with clear service descriptions, local area focus, and Google-friendly structure

### 2. **No Online Booking System**
- **Problem:** Missing calls means missing jobs - no easy way for customers to reach out
- **Solution:** Multiple contact methods (phone, WhatsApp, contact form, postcode checker)

### 3. **Hard to Showcase Work**
- **Problem:** Beautiful landscaping projects have nowhere to be displayed
- **Solution:** Portfolio gallery with 6+ project images, before/after slider, customer testimonials

### 4. **Missing on Google**
- **Problem:** No Google Maps presence means competitors steal local customers
- **Solution:** Integrated service area map, local SEO optimization, Blackpool area focus

## 🎨 Design Personality: Eco-Organic

**Why this design approach?**  
Landscaping is about nature, gardens, and outdoor spaces. The Eco-Organic design personality perfectly captures this with:

### Visual Elements
- **Colors:** Greens (nature), earthy browns, sky blues
- **Border Radius:** 20-30px throughout (soft, organic curves)
- **Typography:** Nunito & Poppins (rounded, friendly fonts)
- **Shadows:** Soft, natural elevation
- **Feel:** Growth, nature, transformation

### User Experience
- Calm, flowing navigation
- Natural color transitions
- Organic shapes and curves
- Nature-inspired imagery
- Trust-building through authenticity
