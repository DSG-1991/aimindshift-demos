## Design Personality: **Friendly Family**

This website uses the **Friendly Family** design personality, characterized by:

### Visual Elements
- **Warm colors:** Blue (#4A90E2), green (#7FBA00), yellow (#FDB714)
- **Soft, rounded corners:** 20-30px border radius
- **Friendly typography:** Nunito (headings), Open Sans (body)
- **Approachable imagery:** Residential homes, happy customers
- **Gentle shadows:** Subtle depth without harshness

### Tone & Messaging
- Conversational, warm language
- Focus on local community ("your neighbors")
- Emphasis on reliability and trust
- Pain point empathy ("We understand your frustrations")
- Family-run business values
