## 🎨 Design Personality: Friendly Family

### Color Palette
| Color | Hex Code | Purpose | Psychology |
|-------|----------|---------|------------|
| Primary (Warm Blue) | #4A90E2 | Headers, icons, links | Trust, cleanliness, professionalism |
| Secondary (Soft Green) | #7FBA00 | Accents, badges | Eco-friendly, freshness, growth |
| Accent (Sunshine Yellow) | #FDB714 | CTAs, highlights | Brightness, positivity, energy |
| Background | #F9F9F9 | Body, cards | Clean, spacious, soft |
| Text Dark | #333 | Body text | Readable, approachable |

### Typography
- **Headings:** Nunito (rounded, friendly, warm)
- **Body:** Open Sans (clean, readable, accessible)
- **Sizes:** 16px minimum (WCAG compliant)

### Shape Language
- **Buttons:** 30px radius (very rounded, inviting)
- **Cards:** 20px radius (gentle, approachable)
- **Images:** 15px radius (soft, warm)

### Shadows
- **Default:** `0 4px 15px rgba(0,0,0,0.1)` (subtle)
- **Hover:** `0 6px 20px rgba(0,0,0,0.15)` (enhanced)
